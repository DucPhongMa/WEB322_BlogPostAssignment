About

And I will do my best to learn it and hope that I can be a fullstack developer in the future.

I am currently attending Seneca College and am in my third year of the Advanced Diploma of Computer Programming and Analysis. During my free time, I usually self-study new technologies related to web development to improve my technical skills. By self-studying, I have gained a fundamental knowledge of ReactJS, Unit Testing as well as understanding how to organize backend and frontend communication in building web-based applications.
